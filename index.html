Welcome

I am currently an Economist at Amazon working in the Customer Behavior Analytics team. I work at the intersection of causal inference and machine learning and I'm passionate about new developments in these fields. Prior to Amazon, I worked at the Inter-American Development Bank (on leave between September 2019 until May, 2020), and a Visiting Assistant Professor at the Department of Quantitative Theory and Methods at Emory University. I obtained a PhD in Economics from the University of Pennsylvania in May, 2017. My research fields are applied microeconomics, labor economics, development economics, and applied econometrics.
